Kinder Connect Feature Directory

Everything each role can do

A quick view of platform capabilities for Admin, School Admin, Staff, and Parent users. Built to help schools understand ownership, responsibilities, and workflows at a glance.

Go to Login Back to Home

Admin

Platform Control

High-level system access for onboarding schools and maintaining platform operations.

  • School creation, update, and lifecycle management
  • Class and section structure planning
  • Student enrollment oversight
  • Circular and announcement management
  • Cross-school visibility through admin dashboards
  • User role governance and operational supervision

School Admin

School Operations

School-focused controls to keep classes, teams, and communication coordinated daily.

  • School dashboard with attendance and activity overview
  • Daily operations monitoring for classes and batches
  • Parent account handling and student-family mapping
  • Review and escalation support for incidents and reports
  • Policy and communication alignment with staff
  • Operational decision support with school metrics

Staff

Teaching Workflow

Teacher and staff tools for student tracking, classroom updates, and parent collaboration.

  • Attendance marking with daily tracking
  • Activity logger for classroom moments
  • Milestone tracking and progress updates
  • Incident reporting with evidence and notes
  • Medical records reference and updates
  • Report builder for monthly development reports
  • Teacher-parent chat and PTM management

Parent

Child Journey

Parent-facing visibility into child development, communication, and school engagement.

  • Parent dashboard for all children under account
  • Child profile with essential records
  • Activity feed with classroom highlights
  • Attendance view for present, absent, and late history
  • Development report and milestone progress view
  • Incident timeline and acknowledgement flow
  • Parent-staff chat and PTM booking support

Kinder Connect - role based transparency for school teams and families.
